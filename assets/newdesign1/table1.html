Software Quality Assurance Engineer
Click Travel -Remote
As a high energy and experienced automation test engineer you will be a positive driving force in representing the customer’s voice in system decisions. You will get a real buzz from overseeing the entire development process, from joining in team planning sessions to signing off the finished product and can work remotely or be office based.

You will be involved in designing, developing and validating automated test scripts using the most appropriate approach to ensure that the software under test is fit for purpose. As a Software QA Engineer you will be curious by nature and have a passion for software testing and test automation, and a sharp eye for detail that will help us continuously improve the quality of our products.

Key responsibilities
  • Assist with the maintenance of our current suite of automation scripts
  • Actively participate in all relevant Agile meetings
  • Maintain quality standards of the products developed by the engineering team
  • Work with developers, business analysts/Product Owners and stakeholders to identify automation candidates to achieve the desired component and end-to-end workflow
  • Keep up to date with industry best practices and provide subject matter expertise
  • Work with the QA team lead to provide continuous enhancements to our processes and products
Requirements:Must haves:
  • Previous significant and relevant experience in software testing;
  • Proficient in Java;
  • Experience with Selenium webdriver;
  • Experience with automated testing methodologies and frameworks;
  • Ability to work well with people from different disciplines with varying degrees of technical experience;
  • Excellent analytical and problem solving skills;
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills;
  • Ability to thrive in a cross-functional team on business critical projects;
  • An insatiable appetite for learning new skills and constantly improving existing ones;
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience.

Nice to haves:
  • Automation experience within DDD;
  • Experience with the JBehave framework;
  • A good understanding of software engineering best practices, object oriented design, and design patterns such as MVC.
Benefits:Key benefits include:
  • Lunch with the founder - everyone has the opportunity to go out for lunch with the founder of the company to chat about things and learn about our vision and future;
  • 1% time - every employee is encouraged to donate 1% of their working time to the community;
  • Travel agent discounts - get access to substantially discounted hotel rates, air tickets and travel tickets.
You can find out about all the benefits we offer on our website.
